Discover the Essence of Zen Rojas

Not sure which Zen Rojas tea will become your favorite? Our convenient sample sizes make it easy to explore our premium blends without the commitment of a full bag. Perfect for discovering new favorites, sharing mindful gifts, or bringing your ritual wherever life takes you.

 

What's Inside

Bodyguard Organic Tea: Crafted with immune-supporting botanicals that help strengthen daily resilience and overall wellbeing.
Organic Heartburn Tea: A soothing herbal infusion formulated to support digestive comfort and help calm acidity after meals.
Premium Sencha Organic Green Tea: A smooth, antioxidant-rich green tea that supports natural energy, focus, and metabolic balance.
Organic Black Tea: A bold, full-bodied classic tea that supports mental alertness and provides a naturally energizing boost.
Organic Sleep Tea: A calming herbal blend designed to promote relaxation and support deeper, more restful sleep.
Unwind Organic Tea: A gentle stress-relief infusion crafted to help ease tension and support emotional balance throughout the day.

 

Why You'll Love It

Small, Mindful Portions — Just enough for a few cups to savor the full character of each blend.

Perfect for On-the-Go — Travel-friendly and effortless to brew anytime, anywhere.
