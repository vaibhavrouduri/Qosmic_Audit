Fortify Your Wellness, Mindfully

Fortify your daily wellness routine with Bodyguard Organic Tea, a carefully crafted loose-leaf blend designed to support your immune system while delivering a refreshing and calming tea experience. Made with 100% organic and ethically sourced ingredients, this naturally caffeinated blend combines the power of botanicals like sencha green tea, ginger, elderflower, linseed, and licorice root to nourish your body and awaken your senses. Whether you’re starting your day or resetting in the afternoon, this tea provides a balanced fusion of flavor, function, and mindfulness. 

 

What's Inside

Organic Herbal Blend: Supports immune health by combining nutrient-rich botanicals that help your body stay resilient and balanced
Sencha Green Tea: Provides natural caffeine that gently boosts energy levels without harsh crashes for sustained daily focus
Ginger And Spearmint: Works together to aid digestion and soothe the body, promoting overall comfort and wellness
Elderflower And Orange Peel: Delivers antioxidant properties that help protect the body from everyday stressors
Linseed And Licorice Root: Helps soothe the digestive system and support gut health while adding natural smoothness to the blend

L-Theanine Balance: Supports a smooth, even energy flow by moderating caffeine, helping you stay calm, focused, and mentally clear throughout the day
Naturally Refreshing Flavor: Combines citrus, herbal, and light sweetness notes for an enjoyable daily ritual

How To Use

Add 2g of loose leaf tea to 8oz hot water

Sip and enjoy daily for best results
