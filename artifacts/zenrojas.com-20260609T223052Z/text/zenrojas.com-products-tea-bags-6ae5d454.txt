Your Perfect Brew, Made Simple

Zen Rojas Tea Bags are designed to be simple, accessible, and enjoyable. These easy-to-use tea bags allow you to fill your own loose leaf blends, giving you full control over strength, flavor, and personal preference without the mess or complexity often associated with loose leaf brewing. Each tin includes 5 tea bags, making it a convenient starter set or a perfect travel-friendly solution for daily tea rituals. Whether you’re new to loose leaf tea or looking for a cleaner brewing method, Zen Rojas makes your perfect cup effortless. 

 

Why You'll Love It

Beginner-friendly
Quick and convenient brewing
Easy disposal after each use
Portable and travel-ready

 

How to Use

Fill tea bag with 2g of loose-leaf tea 

Steep in 8oz hot water for 5–7 minutes 

Dispose tea bag and enjoy your perfect brew
