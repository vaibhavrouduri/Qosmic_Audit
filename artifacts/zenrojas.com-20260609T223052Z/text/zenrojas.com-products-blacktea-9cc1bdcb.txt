Bold Flavor, Mindful Energy

Experience the bold, full-bodied flavor of Organic Black Tea from Zen Rojas, crafted for those who appreciate a rich and energizing daily ritual. Sourced from premium tea gardens in Sri Lanka and India, this 100% organic and ethically sourced loose-leaf tea delivers a naturally caffeinated boost while maintaining a smooth, refined taste. Whether enjoyed as part of your morning routine or an afternoon reset, this tea provides mindful energy and a comforting, aromatic experience.

 

What's Inside

Premium Organic Tea Leaves: Sourced from Sri Lanka and India ensure high-quality flavor and purity, delivering a rich and authentic tea experience
L-Theanine Balance: Supports a smooth, even energy flow by moderating caffeine, helping you stay calm, focused, and mentally clear throughout the day
Full-Bodied Flavor: Creates a bold and satisfying taste, making it ideal for both traditional tea drinkers and new enthusiasts
Aromatic And Smooth Finish: Enhances relaxation and mindfulness, helping you create a calming daily ritual
Versatile Brewing: Work well for hot or iced tea, giving you flexibility to match your daily routine and preferences

 

Why You'll Love It

50 cups (100g) per bag
Premium loose-leaf blend  
Small-batch herbal formulation 
Naturally caffeinated
Ethically sourced ingredients





How to Use

Add 2g of loose leaf tea to 8oz hot water  

Steep for 5–7 minutes 

Sip and enjoy daily
