Drift Into Restful Nights


Unwind naturally and prepare for deep, restorative sleep with the Zen Rojas Organic Sleep Tea. Crafted with a calming blend of chamomile, lemon balm, passionflower, lavender, and skullcap, this organic herbal tea is designed to help you relax your mind, ease tension, and drift into peaceful rest. Each sip delivers a soothing aroma and smooth flavor, making it the perfect nighttime ritual for anyone seeking better sleep without caffeine or harsh ingredients.

 

What's Inside

Organic Herbal Blend: Combines chamomile, lemon balm, passionflower, lavender, and skullcap to naturally calm the body and support restful sleep 
Sleep Support: Offers soothing herbs to help ease stress and promote deeper sleep cycles
Aromatic Floral Profile: Delivers a soothing scent and smooth taste that enhances relaxation before bedtime
Naturally Calming Botanicals: Plant-based alternative to sleep aids without grogginess the next day

 

Why You'll Love It

20 cups (40g) per bag
Premium loose-leaf blend
Small-batch herbal formulation
Caffeine-free

Ethically sourced ingredients

 

How to Use

Add 2g of loose leaf tea to 8oz hot water

Steep for 5–7 minutes  

Sip and enjoy daily for best results
